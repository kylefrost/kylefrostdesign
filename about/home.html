ABOUT KYLE/FROST/DESIGN

Mobile Software Development and Design



Software development is so much more than learning to program and pushing out a few lines of code here
and there. It iss a passion, and it takes passion to create great things. That is what we do. We have a
passion for creating great software that looks great and performs even better. Design is so much more
than making something look pretty, it is making something that feels natural to use. There is a way things
are expected to work that seem so obvious after they are already created, but getting to that point takes
more thought than any end consumer will ever realize. But that is why we do it. To create things that seem
obvious, that seem natural, and that bring an enjoyable experience to the user. That is our goal.
